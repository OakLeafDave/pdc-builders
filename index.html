Our Story

Committed to custom design with superior quality.

PDC Builders is a family-run custom home builder based in Manahawkin, New Jersey, with more than 35 years of experience across Long Beach Island and the Ocean County shore. Led by owner Joe Mellone, we've built our reputation the local way — one satisfied homeowner at a time.
